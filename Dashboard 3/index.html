How to connect a new device?

To connect a new device, go to the Devices section, click on "Add Device", and follow the on-screen instructions. Make sure your device is in pairing mode.

Why is my device offline?

If your device is offline, check if it's powered on and connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your hub. You may need to restart the device.

How to create automation?

Go to the Automations tab, click "Create Automation", select the trigger and action, then save your automation.
